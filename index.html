Deserted milkshakes

Make a nice milkshake


Make a nice milkshake by following these steps and using these ingredients.If you do, your milkshake will taste and look as perfect as your favourite food.To be honest it might even become your favourite food and that is how yummy it is!!Remember if you share this link to anyone you have to say MAKE IT AND DRINK IT!!these recipe's has milk it may have nuts.

prep:1 mins

cook:30 seconds

makes how ever much your blender fits into a glass

ingredients for oreo milkshake

  • 3 oreos
  • 20 ml of milk
  • 5 scoops vanilla ice cream

      • ingredients for snicker's milkshake

      • 1 snicker bar cut into pieces
      • 20 ml of milk
      • 5 scoops of vanilla ice cream

      Method

      STEP 1 for oreo

      Firstly, put 5 scoops of vanilla in a blender.

      STEP 2 for oreo

      Secondly, put the 3 oreo's into the blender.

      STEP 3 for oreo

      Then, put 20 ml of milk inside the blender.

      STEP 4 for oreo

      Lastly, blend it all up and enjoy!


      STEP 1 for snicker's

      Firstly, put 5 scoops of vanilla in a blender.

      STEP 2 for snicker's

      Secondly, put the 3 chopped up snicker's into the blender.

      STEP 3 for snicker's

      Then, put 20 ml of milk inside the blender.

      STEP 4 for snicker's

      Lastly, blend it all up and enjoy!
